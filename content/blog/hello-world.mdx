This is the first post on the rebuilt version of my site. It's now a Next.js app, so I can
write here without hand-editing HTML.

I mostly work on three things:

1. **Deep learning from scratch** — diffusion, JEPA world models, and LLMs reimplemented
   from the papers up in PyTorch.
2. **Vision & perception systems** — surveillance, captioning, and anomaly analysis over
   live video.
3. **Full-stack & tooling** — the apps and developer tools that put the models to work.

I'll use this blog to write up the things that don't fit in a README: the bugs that took a
week, the ideas that didn't pan out, and the occasional one that did. Thanks for reading.
